POSSIBLE is a space for the community to gather, express, and support itself. We are devoted to supporting and facilitating the creativity and wellness of our community. We currently offer a rehearsal space featuring a full backline of gear to support band/music practice. We welcome all musicians and performers seeking a clean and affordable place to practice their art. The space is also available for members of the community to gather and grow: art workshops, book clubs, shared interest meetups, etc.
Cost is $20 per hour with no minimum booking time.
